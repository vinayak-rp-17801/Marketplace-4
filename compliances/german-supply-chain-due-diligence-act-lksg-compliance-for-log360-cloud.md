# German Supply Chain Due Diligence Act (LkSG) Compliance for Log360 Cloud

**Tagline:** Support supply chain IT due diligence obligations under Germany's LkSG with centralized monitoring and reporting from Log360 Cloud.

## Overview
The German Supply Chain Due Diligence Act (LkSG) Compliance extension for Log360 Cloud helps German companies subject to the LkSG meet IT-related risk management, vendor access monitoring, and audit logging obligations for their supply chain due diligence programs. Log360 Cloud monitors access by third-party suppliers and service providers to corporate systems, cloud platforms, and sensitive data repositories, providing security teams with visibility into third-party risk indicators. Compliance teams can track supplier access events, detect anomalous behavior, and generate documentation to support LkSG risk assessment and reporting obligations to BAFA.

## Key Features
- Generate audit reports covering LkSG-relevant IT controls including third-party access monitoring, vendor authentication events, and supply chain risk indicators.
- Monitor access by suppliers and service providers to corporate systems and cloud environments, detecting unauthorized access and policy violations in real time.
- Configure threshold-based alerts for LkSG supply chain risk events such as unusual vendor access patterns, unauthorized data exports, and privilege escalation by third parties.
- Export compliance documentation in PDF and CSV formats for BAFA due diligence reports and internal supply chain risk governance reviews.
